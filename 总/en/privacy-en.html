PRIVACY

DATA COLLECTED

We collect and store necessary information, such as email, password, device type, data usage and your Alipay (or Paypal) account if you have paid, in order to provide our service to you.

We will not store the details of your visiting record.

We will not acquire the details of your communication content. The information you transmit by email or IM tools will be encrypted by default.

DATA SECURITY

When you set a password for your account, your password will be stored encrypted, and the encryption process is irreversible. We do not know what your password is.

Data transferred between your devices and our server has been to encrypted to ensure your data security.

EMAILS

We probably hate spam more than anyone else. Therefore unless it is a big event or a last resort, we will not send you emails.

COMMENTS

We may collect some of the user feedback from emails or third-party websites, and display on the Voyager's website. If you want to update your comments, or do not want to show your comments, please contact us.
